Bastian Köcher

Research associate at the Database Systems and Information Management Group of TU Berlin since November 2016.

I am a PhD candidate supervised by Volker Markl. I received my M.Sc degree at TU Berlin and worked as a student research assistant at DIMA for three years. Prior to that, I received my B.Sc degree at Baden-Württemberg Cooperative State University (DHBW Stuttgart) and worked at IBM for three years.

My research interests include data management, modern hardware and query compilation for heterogeneous processors.
